About Me

I am Joshua Walker, a Final Year Computer Science Student at The University of Pretoria. Out of the range of topics I have been exposed to in my studies, I find Artificial Intelligence and Cloud Computing to be some of the most interesting. I look forward to improving my knowledge and skills in these areas this year and in my Honours programme next year.

Having always been fascinated by Computers and Software, I see them as the primary drivers of innovation and efficiency in our modern world. I believe a successful career in this vast and ever-changing field requires you to embrace a lifetime of learning. I love working with complex systems and solving difficult problems.
